Welcome to the Monmouth County Community Hub. Our digital platform is dedicated to connecting residents with local charities, nonprofits, and community resources. Whether you are looking to volunteer, stay informed about community events, or discover vital support services, our directory and forums are here to help you engage with your community and make a lasting impact.

Community Highlights

Fulfill

Fulfill helps thousands of residents facing food insecurity by providing food through pantries, drives, and partner programs. They also offer nutrition education and emergency support, making them a key resource for families in need.
Website: fulfillnj.org

Shore to Help

Shore to Help is a local charity focused on raising funds for philanthropic causes in Monmouth County. Through their events and fundraisers, they bring the community together to support health, education, and family services.
Website: shore-to-help.com

Friendship Circle of NJ

Friendship Circle provides inclusive programs for individuals with special needs. By pairing them with teen volunteers, they foster genuine friendships and offer a welcoming environment for social, recreational, and educational growth.
Website: fcnj.com
