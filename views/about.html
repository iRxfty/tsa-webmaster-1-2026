About Monmouth County

Monmouth is located in New Jersey and is most commonly known for the Battle of Monmouth during the American Revolution. It is one of the original East Jersey counties. It was established in 1683 and has a long history, including early Quaker settlements and its more recent transition into a coastal community. The county consists of 53 municipalities, including townships, boroughs, and cities.
